about

I deliberately made this website. Eschewing extravagant styles and superfluous functionalities, with the fundamental belief that a website serves as a conduit for efficient information dissemination. By adhering to simplicity, I aim to optimize accessibility among as more number of computers as possible, including those with modest hardware specifications. This peculiar design choice is underpinned by a profound recognition of the ubiquitous nature of information consumption in the digital age. Recognizing the vast array of devices and varying technological capabilities employed by users worldwide, I have endeavored to create an interface that transcends the boundaries of technological disparity, ensuring seamless access to content across a broad spectrum of computing environments. In essence, my commitment to simplicity is not merely a stylistic preference, but a principled stance rooted in the philosophy of inclusivity, where the dissemination of information takes precedence over ostentatious design elements.
