Sneak

Type: ~
Release time: 2019
Family: Hightype (11S)

Sneak is a neo-grotesque typeface, featuring several reversed characters, such as its distinctive S. Sneak is available in five weights with corresponding Italics as well as a monospaced style.
This font is characterized by reversing upside-down characters like "B", "S", "3" ,"8", which have unequal widths at the top and bottom.
